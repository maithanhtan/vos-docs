Put Through is the workspace for PT intent filtering, advertisement review, and PT order tracking.

## Full page

![Put Through full page](/images/vero-oms-ui/put-through-full.png)

## PT Workspace Filters

The filter sidebar contains symbol, side, status, account, and IOI ID controls.

![Put Through filters](/images/vero-oms-ui/put-through-filters.png)

## Advertisements

The advertisements area displays status tabs, open IOI count, matched count, and notional context.

![Put Through advertisements](/images/vero-oms-ui/put-through-advertisements.png)

## PT Orders

The PT orders area displays the put-through order queue after filters return matching data.

![Put Through PT orders](/images/vero-oms-ui/put-through-pt-orders.png)
