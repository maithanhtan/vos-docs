Portfolios provides valuation, exposure, positions, allocation, and concentration data for the selected account context.

## Workspace overview

![Portfolios workspace overview](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-full.png)

## Portfolio tabs

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Cash Balance | Reviews account cash, holds, exposure, and cash/stock check status. |
| Positions | Reviews holdings, live marks, P&L, allocation, concentration, and movers. |
| What-if · stress | Runs scenario, Monte Carlo, and historical VaR views against the current book. |

## KPI Summary

The KPI strip displays market value, P&L, day P&L, open position count, and available cash.

![Portfolio KPI summary](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-kpis.png)

## Positions Table

The positions table lists symbol, account, quantity, sellable quantity, average cost, mark, market value, day P&L, and weight.

![Portfolio positions table](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-positions-table.png)

| Control | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Asset-class tabs | Filters positions by all holdings, equities, derivatives, cash-like assets, or other configured classes. |
| Account search | Narrows positions to one account. |
| Sector selector | Narrows positions to one sector. |
| Header sort | Sorts visible rows by symbol, name, class, sector, account, quantity, sellable, cost, mark, value, P&L, or weight. |
| Table footer | Shows the displayed range and total matching positions. |

## What-if and cash states

| Area | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Cash Balance | Sorts account rows by account, cash balance, cash hold, stock exposure, and risk-check flags. |
| Scenario mode | Switches among limit-down, limit-up, uniform shock, sector shock, Monte Carlo, and historical VaR. |
| Uniform shock | Applies preset percentage moves to all holdings. |
| Sector stress | Enables editable sector shock inputs in the right rail. |
| Monte Carlo | Changes volatility and tail assumptions, then shows distribution statistics. |
| Historical VaR | Changes lookback period and uses available daily history for the top holdings. |
