Portfolios provides valuation, exposure, positions, allocation, and concentration data for the selected account context.

## Full page

![Portfolios full page](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-full.png)

## KPI Summary

The KPI strip displays market value, P&L, day P&L, open position count, and available cash.

![Portfolio KPI summary](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-kpis.png)

## Positions Table

The positions table lists symbol, account, quantity, sellable quantity, average cost, mark, market value, day P&L, and weight.

![Portfolio positions table](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-positions-table.png)

## Allocation

The allocation panel displays exposure by asset class, account, sector, and top concentration.

![Portfolio allocation](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-allocation.png)

## Treemaps

Treemaps classify top decliners and top gainers for the selected portfolio context.

![Portfolio treemaps](/images/vero-oms-ui/portfolios-treemaps.png)
