Operation contains account administration, risk rules, approvals, reference actions, and operational search.

## Full page

![Operation full page](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-full.png)

## Tabs and actions

The action bar contains Accounts, Risk Rules, Approvals, search, New Group, Add Account, Reference Data, and Refresh.

![Operation tabs and actions](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-tabs-actions.png)

## Account Table

The account table displays account id, name, type, group, margin, short sell, users, and rules.

![Operation account table](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-account-table.png)

## Account Detail

The right-side account panel displays and manages the selected account.

![Operation account detail](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-account-detail.png)

## Risk Rules

Risk Rules contains the rule catalog, filters by engine/scope/kind, rules list, and rule detail panel.

![Operation risk rules](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-risk-rules.png)

## Approvals

Approvals contains review queues, request state, and the selected approval detail panel.

![Operation approvals](/images/vero-oms-ui/operation-approvals.png)
